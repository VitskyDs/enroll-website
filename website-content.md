## 4. Solution / Value Prop Section

**Goal:** Introduce Enroll as the answer.

- **Headline:** "Your brand. Your program. Powered by AI."
- Short paragraph: Enroll combines loyalty rewards and subscription billing into one intelligent platform — designed specifically for service businesses. It learns your brand and builds programs that actually retain customers.

Four differentiator tiles (visual icons + short copy):

- 🤖 **AI-First Personalization** — Programs built around your business, not a template
- 💳 **Loyalty + Subscriptions** — Recurring revenue and retention in one place
- 🏪 **Built for Service Businesses** — From salons to pet care and beyond
- 🏆 **Enterprise Playbook, SMB Price** — The Starbucks strategy, without the enterprise bill

## 5. How It Works

**Goal:** Reduce friction and show simplicity. Three steps.

1. **Tell us about your business** — Enroll's AI learns your brand, customers, and goals
2. **Launch your custom program** — Loyalty tiers, rewards, and subscriptions go live — no code
3. **Watch retention grow** — AI continuously optimizes your program as data comes in
